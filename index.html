Welcome to First Baptist Church West End

About Us

West End First Baptist Church is a community of believers dedicated to sharing the love of Jesus Christ in Roatan, Honduras. We strive to be a beacon of hope and faith in our community through worship, service, and fellowship.

Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 9:30 AM for Sunday School and 11:00 AM for our Worship Service.

Weekly Bible Study

Ladies Bible Study is at 6 PM on Mondays and Men's Bible Study is at 7 PM Tuesdays. All are welcome!
