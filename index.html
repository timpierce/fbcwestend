About Us

West End First Baptist Church is a community of believers dedicated to sharing the love of Jesus Christ in Roatan, Honduras. We strive to be a beacon of hope and faith in our community through worship, service, and fellowship.

Sunday Services

Join us for Sunday School at 9:30 AM and Worship Service at 11 AM. Wednesday night services are at 7 PM.

Bible Study

We have Ladies Bible Study on Monday nights at 6 PM and Men's Bible Study on Tuesday nights at 7 PM.
